FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sky Nexus?

Sky Nexus is a 2D pixel-art action and adventure run-and-gun game. Explore artificial planets, face biomechanical enemies, and dive into an epic science-fiction story set in the year 5605.

On which platforms will Sky Nexus be available?

The game will be released for PC (Steam, Epic Games, Itch.io), Nintendo Switch, Xbox and PlayStation.

Do I need to pay to play the demo?

No! The Sky Nexus demo is completely free and allows you to experience a few stages and mechanics before the official release.

Can I play in English or Japanese?

Yes! Sky Nexus offers support for multiple languages, including Portuguese, English and Japanese.

Will Sky Nexus have multiplayer?

No. Sky Nexus is a single-player game, focused on a narrative experience and individual action, allowing you to explore the story and mechanics at your own pace.

What are the minimum PC requirements?

Operating System: Windows 10
Processor: Intel i3 / AMD Ryzen 3
RAM: 4GB
Graphics Card: Intel HD 4000 or equivalent
Storage: 2GB

How does the weapon system work?

NanoRector 9: basic single-shot weapon with infinite ammunition. Tri-Shot: fires 3 cone-shaped projectiles to hit multiple enemies. PlasmaBeam: continuous plasma beam that deals high damage. Raptor-Lock / Viper-M: missiles that track enemies. FlameBurst: unleashes continuous flames using animation prefabs. You can switch weapons by collecting power-ups scattered throughout the levels.

Does the game have drones or companions?

Yes! Emily is accompanied by drones called Companions, each with specific functions: Satori: analyses the environment and predicts enemy routes. Inori: protective and sensitive to Emily’s condition, provides defensive support. Kagero: an aggressive drone designed for quick attacks. Hikari: illuminates areas and helps detect hidden enemies.

How do power-ups and upgrades work?

You collect power-ups to: Change weapons. Recharge energy.

Which controls are supported?

Keyboard and mouse: standard for PC. Gamepad: compatible with controllers such as PS5, Xbox and others. Shooting and movement are independent, allowing free aiming while moving.

Will I receive game updates after purchasing?

Yes! Everyone who purchases the digital copy will have access to future updates at no additional cost.
